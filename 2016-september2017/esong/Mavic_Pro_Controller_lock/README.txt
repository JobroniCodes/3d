# Summary

Mavic Pro controller lock for transport and storage.
Will hold your controllers gimbals in place near center so they don't get forced against the stops while storing or transporting your Mavic sUAS

# Print Settings

Printer: Monoprice Select Mini 3D Printer with Heated Build Plate
Rafts: No
Supports: No
Resolution: .1
Infill: 20%

Notes: 
I printed it out of PLA. Fits and works as it should.
